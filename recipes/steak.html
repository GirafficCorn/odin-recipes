Steak

Description

The recipe is very simple and involves a reverse sear method. We will smoke the steak at 225 degrees in our Traeger followed by pan searing it in our cast iron.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Avocado oil

Steps

  1. Pat steak dry with paper towel
  2. Season both sides with salt and pepper
  3. Smoke steak at 225 degrees until an internal temperature of 100 degrees
  4. Heat cast iron pan and remove steak from Traeger
  5. Add oil to pan and sear steak on high heat for 1 minute each side
